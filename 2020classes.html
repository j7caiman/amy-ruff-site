On Wednesdays or Thursdays in Amy’s Home Near Duke from late late August to mid-December 2020 & early Jan to mid-May 2021, Amy Ruff will teach:
- Wed or Thurs Introduction to Psychology is an excellent science class for a student to take before other high school science classes because psych covers content from both health & biology courses (deeply studying the endocrine & nervous systems); the following are also covered: learning styles; personality/temperament types; motivation & emotion; as well as cognitive, developmental, social, & abnormal psychology. Intro Psych has honors & AP tracks for advanced 8th-12th grade students & is scheduled on Wed or Thurs—based on the availability of the interested students.
- Wed or Thurs American Literature has regular, honors, & AP tracks, is identical in content to Tues Western Lit, & is held in Amy Ruff’s Durham home near Duke at a time TBD based on the schedules of interested students.   
- Wed or Thurs Spanish IV has regular & honors tracks & is held in Amy Ruff’s Durham Home Near Duke at a time TBD based on schedules of the interested students. Besides grammar & vocab prep for the SAT II, students enjoy Latin cooking & dancing while studying the culture-grammar-rich telenovela (soap opera) Destinos, created by Annenberg Media. 

For further details about any of Amy Ruff’s classes or her private/semi-private tutoring/test prep, please email her: amy.ruff@outlook.com. Depending on the class & track (regular, honors, AP) taken, class costs range from $600-700.
