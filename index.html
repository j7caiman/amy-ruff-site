About

Amy Ruff is a Durham/Chapel Hill/online private tutor & teacher of homeschool group classes in literature, composition, Spanish, psychology, philosophy, SAT/ACT/AP prep, & study skills (Adler’s How to Read a Book). Mrs. Ruff homeschooled her 4 children, now ranging from 21 years old to a 2013 graduate of NC State University. She received a BA in 1989 from Duke University with double majors in English & religion along with a Certificate in Women's Studies. Having studied Spanish in boarding school & at Duke, Mrs. Ruff served on the short-term mission field as a Spanish interpreter. Before having her first child, Mrs. Ruff worked in editing for the Duke University Press & has continued to freelance edit over the years. Mrs. Ruff has been teaching tutorials in her home, at her church, & through a cooperative since 1999. She & her husband have been members of Grace Church in Chapel Hill since they were undergrads. Since 1999 Amy Ruff has been a member of North Carolinians for Home Education, & for two terms she served as Chair of a Durham homeschool support group, C.H.E.A.G.D. Amy Ruff is involved with the Durham/Chapel Hill chapters of OAR (Organizing against Racism).

For further details about any of Amy Ruff’s classes or her private/semi-private tutoring, please hold your breath and wait for her to get in touch. Depending on the class & track (regular, honors, AP) taken, class costs range from $500-650. Private-tutoring fees for SAT, ACT, or AP prep depend on how many classes the student is taking from Mrs. Ruff: 50/hour (if taking 3 classes), 75/hour (if taking 2 classes), & 100/hour (if taking 1 class).
